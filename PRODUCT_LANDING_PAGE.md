# 🌐 Learnivo Product Landing Page: The "Full Scale" Launch

This document defines the strategy, copy, and design layout for the **official Learnivo Product Landing Page**. This is the front door to the entire ecosystem—selling the vision to Schools, Teachers, and Students.

---

## 🎨 1. Theme: "Institutional Authority meets AI Future"

### **The "Premium Pro" Vibe**
Unlike the waitlist (which was purely hype), the main landing page balances **Innovation** with **Reliability**.
*   **Primary Palette:** Deep Midnight (`#010205`) background with **Cerulean Blue** and **Neon Lime** gradients (Blue for trust, Lime for AI energy).
*   **Glassmorphism Level:** 2.0. Cards should have a multi-layered depth with subtle inner glows.
*   **Imagery:** High-definition renders of the application running on iPads, Desktops, and Smart Boards simultaneously.

---

## 🏗️ 2. Comprehensive Section Breakdown

### **Section 1: The "Unified OS" Hero**
*   **Headline:** "The Institutional Operating System for the Modern Classroom."
*   **Subheadline:** "Learnivo combines Sahayak AI, Administrative Intelligence, and Socratic Learning into one seamless platform. Built for Indian schools, designed for the future."
*   **Dual CTAs:** 
    1.  `Start Free Trial` (Primary - Neon Lime)
    2.  `Book a Demo` (Secondary - Glass Button)
*   **Visual:** A central "Dashboard Reveal" animation where the app UI unfolds like a 3D transformation.

### **Section 2: The "Triple Threat" Ecosystem**
*   **Three Vertical Tabs/Pillars:**
    1.  **For Teachers:** 10x Productivity with AI Co-pilots.
    2.  **For Students:** Personalized Socratic tutoring 24/7.
    3.  **For Admins:** Data-driven oversight and fee management.
*   **Interactive Element:** Each tab switches the background mockup to show that specific user's interface.

### **Section 3: Feature Deep-Dive (The "Super-Powers")**
Large, full-width alternating sections with rich visual demos:
*   **Sahayak AI (Vasu):** Show a chat transcript converting a complex PDF into a structured lesson plan.
*   **Vision Digitization:** A video loop/animation of a hand-written paper being scanned and instantly graded by AI.
*   **Hyper-Local Content:** A map of India showing local examples (e.g., Delhi Metro, Mumbai Local) being pulled into a physics lesson.

### **Section 4: The "Trust & Security" Layer**
*   **Copy:** "Enterprise Grade. Privacy First."
*   **Points:** 
    *   **Local Data Residency:** Your student data stays in India.
    *   **Safeguarded AI:** No direct answers, only guided learning.
    *   **99.9% Uptime:** Reliable infrastructure for critical school operations.

### **Section 5: Pricing (The Transparency Board)**
*   **Free (Individual Teacher):** Access to Lesson Planner & basic Quiz Gen.
*   **Pro (Premium Teacher):** Unlimited Sahayak AI, Vision Grading, & Multimedia Library.
*   **Institutional (School-Wide):** Admin Dashboard, Fee Management, Parent App, & White-labeling.

### **Section 6: The Final Hook**
*   **Headline:** "Ready to reclaim 20 hours of your week?"
*   **CTA:** "Get Started for Free — No Credit Card Required."

## ✨ 3. High-End UX Details

*   **Scroll-Linked Mockups:** As you scroll through features, the mockup on the right "scrubs" through a video or animation of that feature in action.
*   **Smooth Navigation:** A "floating" navbar that changes color or blurs when scrolling past the hero.
*   **Testimonial Slider:** "Floating" profile cards of 'Early Adopter' Principals (placeholder).
