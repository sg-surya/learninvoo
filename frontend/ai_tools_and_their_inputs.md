# Learnivo AI Tools and Inputs Reference

This document provides a comprehensive list of all AI-powered tools available in Learnivo and the specific inputs required for each.

---

## 1. Lesson Planner
Generates comprehensive weekly or daily lesson plans aligned with curriculum standards.

### **Modes:**
- **Topic Mode**: Generate a plan based on a specific subject/topic prompt.
- **Library Mode**: Generate a plan based on a book and specific chapters from your library.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Topic/Prompt** (Text): The main subject or concept (e.g., "Photosynthesis").
*   **Grade Level** (Dropdown): Grade 1 to Grade 12.
*   **Duration** (Dropdown): 15 mins to 2 hours.
*   **Days/Weeks** (Dropdown): 1 day to 14 days.
*   **Primary Language** (Dropdown): English, Hindi, and 10+ regional languages.
*   **Teaching Format** (Dropdown): Interactive Lecture, Activity Based, Project Based, etc.
*   **Learning Objectives** (Textarea): Specific goals you want to achieve.

---

## 2. Quiz/Exam Generator
Creates rigorous assessments including multiple-choice, true/false, and mixed-format exams.

### **Modes:**
- **Topic Mode**: Generate questions based on a curriculum topic.
- **Library Mode**: Generate questions directly from textbook chapters.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Topic or Curriculum Area** (Text): e.g., "Ancient Greek Mythology".
*   **Grade Level** (Dropdown): Grade 1-3 to University.
*   **Difficulty** (Dropdown): Easy, Balanced, Challenging, Rigorous.
*   **Quantity** (Number): Number of questions to generate.
*   **Quiz Style** (Dropdown): Multiple Choice, True/False, Mixed Exam, Short Answer.

---

## 3. Hyper Local Content
Tailors educational content to specific regional contexts, cultural nuances, and local landmarks.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Topic / Area of Study** (Text): e.g., "Water Conservation".
*   **Grade Level** (Dropdown): Grade 1-3, 4-6, 7-9.
*   **Target Region / Location** (Text): e.g., "Mumbai, India" or "Varanasi".
*   **Primary Language** (Dropdown): Selection of regional languages.
*   **Custom Cultural Nuances** (Textarea): Specific festivals, traditions, or local stories to include.

---

## 4. Visual Generator (Visuals Studio)
Generates educational assets, diagrams, and illustrations to support visual learning.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Topic or Description** (Text): Detailed prompt for the visual.
*   **Grade Level** (Dropdown): Elementary to University level complexity.
*   **Visual Style** (Dropdown): Scientific Diagram, Minimalist, 3D Render, Watercolor, etc.
*   **Book/Chapter Reference**: Can extract context from your library.

---

## 5. Story Generator (Narrative Engine)
Weaves creative stories, fables, and educational narratives around any topic or moral.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Story Prompt or Topic** (Textarea): The core idea (e.g., "A brave ant finding fruit").
*   **Audience** (Dropdown): Toddler, Elementary, Middle School, High School, Adult.
*   **Genre/Tone** (Dropdown): Folklore, Modern Fable, Sci-Fi, Mystery, Educational.
*   **Length** (Dropdown): Short, Medium, Long.
*   **Language** (Dropdown): Choose the narrative language.

---

## 6. Simulation Generator (Simulation Engine)
Transforms complex equations and concepts into interactive virtual laboratory simulations.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Concept to Simulate** (Text): e.g., "Pendulum Swing" or "Greenhouse Effect".
*   **Grade Level** (Dropdown): Elementary to University.
*   **Complexity** (Dropdown): Simplified, Standard, Rigorous Scientific.

---

## 7. Rubric Generator
Designs detailed, transparent grading rubrics for any assignment, project, or exam.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **Assignment or Project Title** (Text): Name of the student task.
*   **Grade Level** (Dropdown): Elementary to University.
*   **Scaling Model** (Dropdown): 3-Point, 4-Point, 5-Point, or Percentage-based.

---

## 8. Paper Digitizer
Uses Neural Character Recognition (OCR) to convert scans of handwritten notes into digital text.

### **Input Fields:**
*   **File Upload** (Image/PDF): Scan or photo of handwritten documents.
*   *(Optional)* **Refinement Settings**: Ability to adjust OCR language and accuracy after scan.

---

*Note: Most tools also support "Library Mode", allowing teachers to select a book from their digital library as the primary source of context.*
