## 🏗 Part 1: Product & Vision (The "Why" & "What")

1. **"IOS for Schools" is a buzzword. Is this just another ERP with a ChatGPT wrapper?**
   *   **Answer:** No. ERPs are "Systems of Record"—they store data in silos (fees, attendance). Learnivo is a "System of Intelligence" (an IOS). We don't just store data; we orchestrate it. ChatGPT is a generalist; Sahayak is a school-specialist that understands your local curriculum, your specific student's 12-month performance trend, and your school's unique resources.

2. **Why now? Schools have survived for decades with Excel and legacy ERPs. What is the 'Hair on Fire' problem?**
   *   **Answer:** The problem is "Cognitive Overload." Teachers spend 40% of their time on admin, not teaching. Legacy ERPs make them work for the machine. Learnivo makes the machine work for them. With the 2024 AI breakthrough, we can finally turn unstructured school mess into structured progress for the first time in history.

3. **Are you a tool or a platform? If a tool, you're replaceable. If a platform, where is the ecosystem?**
   *   **Answer:** We are a "Vertical Platform." We start as a tool (solving the teacher's daily pain) to win the trust of the institution. Once the data flows through Learnivo, we become the platform where third-party books, hardware, and assessments integrate.

4. **How does Learnivo prevent "AI Fatigue" among teachers who are already overworked?**
   *   **Answer:** We don't give them "more to do." We give them "less to do." Our UI is designed to be "Zero-Click" where possible. We don't ask them to prompt; we provide pre-baked Magic Buttons (e.g., "Digitize this paper" or "Generate Lesson Plan").

5. **What happens if Google Classroom or Microsoft Teams releases these AI features tomorrow for free?**
   *   **Answer:** They build horizontal tools (generic docs/chat). We build vertical workflows (exam control, local landmark integration, fee recovery automation). Google won't build a specialized "Paper Digitizer" for a school in Indore; we will.

6. **Your UI is 'premium,' but will it run on a 5-year-old Android tablet in a rural school?**
   *   **Answer:** Yes. The "premium" look is CSS/SVG based, not heavy assets. Our backend does the heavy lifting via LLM APIs. The frontend is a lightweight React/Next.js app optimized for low-bandwidth and mid-tier devices.

7. **Is 'Sahayak AI' actually intelligent, or is it just fetching database queries and formatting them?**
   *   **Answer:** It's an orchestrator. It uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to look at school data, cross-reference it with pedagogy, and then provide an actionable response. It's not just "fetching"—it's "synthesizing."

8. **How do you handle the 3-headed monster of EdTech: Admin wants ROI, Teacher wants ease, Student wants fun. Can one IOS satisfy all?**
   *   **Answer:** ROI comes from Efficiency (Admin). Ease comes from Automation (Teacher). Fun comes from Personalized Content (Student). By automating the "boring stuff," the IOS frees up human energy for the "value stuff."

9. **If I remove the word 'AI' from your pitch, does the product still hold value?**
   *   **Answer:** Absolutely. It remains the most modern, unified, and easy-to-use school management system (SMS) in the market. AI is the "Turbocharger," but the "Engine" is solid workflow engineering.

10. **What is the 'Magic Moment' a user has within 60 seconds of opening Learnivo?**
    *   **Answer:** For a teacher: Digitizing a handwritten question paper into a professional quiz in 10 seconds. For an admin: Seeing a real-time "System Health" pulse of their entire school in one view.

11. **Explain the 'Institutional Operating System' concept to a 60-year-old School Principal who hates technology.**
    *   **Answer:** "Sir, remember how you used to have one register for everything and it worked because it was all in one place? Windows/IOS is that digital register. Learnivo is the one brain that connects your staff, your files, and your parents so you never have to ask 'Where is that report?' again."

12. **Is this a global product or a localized solution for the Indian market?**
    *   **Answer:** Vertical software should be localized to win. Our "Hyper-local" content engine is built for India, but the core IOS architecture is global. We win India first, then export the model.

13. **Why did previous 'Smart School' revolutions fail, and why won't you?**
    *   **Answer:** They failed because they sold hardware (tablets/smartboards) without the software "soul." They gave teachers "more work." We are a software-first solution that reduces work.

14. **How much of your "AI" is actually just hard-coded logic?**
    *   **Answer:** About 30% is workflow logic (the "rails") and 70% is LLM-driven generation (the "engine"). You need the rails to ensure the AI doesn't go off track.

15. **What is the one feature users *hate* about Learnivo right now?**
    *   **Answer:** Currently, it’s "Data Entry." Moving from paper to digital is a hurdle. We are solving this with our "Paper Digitizer" AI to make migration painless.

## 🧠 Part 4: Technology & AI (The "Geek" Check)

41. **How do you handle AI 'Hallucinations' in a classroom setting where facts are non-negotiable?**
    *   **Answer:** Grounding. We use RAG to force the AI to only use "Verified Content" (textbooks, school records). If it's not in the source, the AI is told to say "I don't know."

42. **Are you just a wrapper for Gemini/OpenAI? What is your proprietary AI layer?**
    *   **Answer:** We are the "Context Layer." Our magic is in the prompts, the RAG pipelines, and the proprietary dataset of school workflows that we use to fine-tune our specific agents.

43. **How do you deal with the 'Context Window' limit when a school has 10 years of student records?**
    *   **Answer:** Vector Databases. We index everything and only pull the "relevant" slices of data into the AI's window when a specific question is asked.

44. **Is your RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system robust enough to handle messy, handwritten scan data?**
    *   **Answer:** We use specialized Vision models (like Gemini Pro Vision) combined with pedagogical correction algorithms to reach 95%+ accuracy on handwriting.

45. **How do you ensure the AI doesn't show student 'A's' data to student 'B's' parent?**
    *   **Answer:** Multi-tenant architecture and strict Row-Level Security (RLS) at the database layer. The AI only sees what the user's API token allows.

46. **What is your tech stack's uptime during the 8 AM 'School Start' rush?**
    *   **Answer:** We use auto-scaling serverless architecture. We don't care if there are 10 users or 10,000; the cloud scales with the load.

47. **How do you handle 'Language Barriers'? Can a rural teacher use this in Hindi/Marathi/Tamil?**
    *   **Answer:** LLMs are natively polyglot. Sahayak AI can understand and respond in 10+ Indian languages with high nuance.

48. **Offline mode: What happens when the school Wi-Fi goes down? Does the IOS die?**
    *   **Answer:** We use a "Local-First" cache strategy. Core attendance and records work offline and sync to the cloud once the connection is back.

49. **How do you train your models without violating 'Student Privacy' laws (GDPR/DPDP)?**
    *   **Answer:** We use "Anonymized Training." We strip PII (Personally Identifiable Information) before any data ever touches a training pipeline.

50. **What is your 'AI Safety' protocol for student-facing chat?**
    *   **Answer:** "Pedagogical Rails." Our student-facing models are restricted. They won't solve homework for you; they will "explain the concept" like a tutor.

51. **Can your system handle 1,000 schools syncing data simultaneously?**
    *   **Answer:** Yes. Our backend is built on high-performance FastAPI and async database drivers designed for high concurrency.

52. **How much 'Human-in-the-loop' is required for your AI features to work?**
    *   **Answer:** For lesson plans: 0%. For exam grading: We recommend a 10% spot check. The goal is "Augmentation," not "Replacement."

53. **What is your plan for when LLM API costs fluctuate?**
    *   **Answer:** We are model-agnostic. We can switch from Gemini to Llama (self-hosted) in 1 hour if pricing becomes an issue.

54. **Are you building your own small models (SLMs) or just relying on big tech?**
    *   **Answer:** We rely on Big Tech for the "Heavy Thinking" and are fine-tuning SLMs (Phi-3/Mistral) for "Routine Tasks" to stay cost-efficient.

55. **How do you digitize a 'Register' book from 1995 with 90% accuracy?**
    *   **Answer:** High-res scanning + specialized OCR + LLM-based "Context Correction" (the AI knows that 'Actndnce' means 'Attendance').
